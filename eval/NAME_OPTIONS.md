# Project Name Options — Analysis

## Naming Criteria

The name must:
- Be metaphorical, not descriptive
- Sound like a real organism/object, not an acronym
- Carry the meaning of "autonomous networked agent that acts"
- Be memorable, short, pronounceable in any language
- Have no trademark conflicts in the AI space (as of April 2026)

## Shortlisted Names

---

### 1. AXON
**Metaphor:** The axon is the long projection of a neuron that carries signals from the brain to the body — the literal wire between thought and action.

Perfect for an agent that connects AI reasoning (the brain) to real-world execution (the body). An axon does not think. It transmits. It acts. It connects everything.

- Phonetics: clean, two syllables, works in English, French, Spanish, Yoruba, Portuguese
- Domain: axon.ai likely taken but axonai, getaxon, axonagent available
- Vibe: scientific, precise, fast, biological
- Tagline: *"The wire between thought and action."*

---

### 2. MYCEL
**Metaphor:** Mycelium — the underground fungal network that connects trees, transfers nutrients, sends signals across entire forests. Invisible, distributed, always running, connects everything.

An agent that runs in the background, connects all your platforms, routes information invisibly — that IS mycelium.

- Phonetics: MY-sel — clean, two syllables
- Domain: mycel.ai, mycel.dev likely available
- Vibe: organic, distributed, persistent, underground infrastructure
- Tagline: *"The invisible network that connects everything."*

---

### 3. DURA
**Metaphor:** Dura mater — the tough outermost membrane protecting the brain. The hardened shell that lets intelligence operate safely inside. Strong, protective, always present.

An agent with a security-first architecture that protects everything it touches.

- Phonetics: DOO-rah — clean, strong
- Domain: dura.ai possibly available
- Vibe: protective, resilient, serious, trustworthy
- Tagline: *"The hardened layer between intelligence and the world."*

---

### 4. GANGL
**Metaphor:** Ganglia — clusters of neurons outside the brain that process signals autonomously. They handle reflexes, routing, and background signals without the central brain's involvement.

An agent with autonomous background processing, multi-agent routing, watch daemons — all running like ganglia.

- Phonetics: GANG-el — unusual enough to be memorable
- Domain: likely available
- Vibe: technical, neural, autonomous, slightly alien
- Tagline: *"Autonomous signal processing at every layer."*

---

### 5. SYNAP
**Metaphor:** The synapse — the gap between neurons where signals jump, where connection happens, where intelligence transfers between systems.

An agent that bridges everything: AI to action, phone to desktop, thought to execution.

- Phonetics: SIN-ap — clean
- Tagline: *"Where intelligence becomes action."*

---

## Recommendation

**AXON** is the strongest choice.

| Criterion | AXON | MYCEL | DURA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Metaphor strength | ★★★★★ | ★★★★★ | ★★★★ |
| Pronounceability | ★★★★★ | ★★★★ | ★★★★★ |
| Memorability | ★★★★★ | ★★★★ | ★★★★ |
| Available domains | ★★★ | ★★★★★ | ★★★★ |
| Meaning fit | ★★★★★ | ★★★★★ | ★★★★ |

AXON captures the exact nature of this system: the neural pathway that connects thinking to doing. It's short, scientific, universal, and carries no existing AI brand baggage.

**MYCEL** is the strongest alternative — more unusual, better domain availability, carries the distributed/background-running metaphor perfectly.
